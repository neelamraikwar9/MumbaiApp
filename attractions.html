Top Attractions in Mumbai

Gateway of India

A historic monument built the British Raj, overlooking the Arabian Sea.

Location: Apollo Bunder, Colaba


Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus(CST)

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, a historic railway station with stunning architecture.

Location: Fort, Mumbai


Marine Drive

A picturesque promenade along the Arabian Sea, known as the Queen's Necklace.

Location: Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose Road, South Mumbai
