Mumbai City Cuisine

Mumbai is a culinary paradise with a diverse range of flavors. Here are some must-try dishes and food experiences in the city:

Pav Bhaji

A popular street food dish consisting of a spicy vegetable curry(bhaji ) served with soft bread rolls(pav).


Bhel Puri

A delightful combination of puffed rice, sev, chutneys, and various ingredients, creating a tasty snack.


Vada Pav

Known as the "Indian Burger," vada pav consists of a spicy potato fritter(vada) placed inside a pav.
